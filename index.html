A few months ago she realized that she cannot see well. She is afraid to use the stove and she seldom goes to the Supermarket. A young man from the neighborhood, who was not working, volunteered to assist with the shopping, the cleaning of the house and the yard. He did this for about six months. She even allowed him to change the pension cheque a few times, but now she does not answer the door when he comes, although she still hears very well. She now refuses help from anyone and she does not socialize. You are the Social Worker assigned to the case. On your first visit you observe marks on her hands (like scratches), she is very withdrawn, she is very frail, and the house is dirty, smelly and stuffy. She offers you a cigarette (which you kindly refuse) as she lights another one for herself. Throughout the interview she is coughing very much and she is very weepy. She tells you she cannot remember when last she had a cooked meal. She also says that when her husband was alive they always ate together and that she wishes she had died with him.

  • Identify 2 indications thatMrs.Bob misses her husband.
  • State 2 reasons why you think Mrs.bob was coughing very much.
